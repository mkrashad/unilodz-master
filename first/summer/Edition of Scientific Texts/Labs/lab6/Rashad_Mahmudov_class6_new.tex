\exercise{Exercise 1.}

\begin{table}[h]
\centering
\begin{tabular}[t]{|r|c|l|}
 \hline
 $A$ & $B$ & $C$ \\ 
 \hline
 $a$ & $b$ & $c$ \\
 \hline
 $a-b$ & $2b+c_1$ & $c_1-a_1$ \\ 
 \hline
 $a_1$ & $b_1$ & $c$ \\ 
 \hline
\end{tabular}
\caption{First table}
\end{table}
Remember, that when you write \emph{A1} you have to use math mode and write
\verb|$A_1$|. Separate symbol \verb|_| we can obtain writing \verb|\_|.
